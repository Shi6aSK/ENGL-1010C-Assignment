Welcome to Quantum Computing!

This website provides an introduction to quantum computing for undergraduate students with a computer science background. No advanced physics or mathematics required!

Introduction to Quantum Computing

Quantum computing leverages quantum mechanics to solve problems more efficiently than classical computers, impacting fields like cryptography and AI.

Why Quantum Computing?

Quantum computers can solve specific complex problems that would take classical computers years. They are critical in optimizing complex systems and handling massive data.
