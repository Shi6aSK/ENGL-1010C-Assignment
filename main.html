Welcome to Quantum Computing!

This website provides an introduction to quantum computing for undergraduate students with a computer science background. You don't need to have an advanced understanding of physics or mathematics to get started here.

Why Quantum Computing?

Quantum computing is a revolutionary field that leverages the principles of quantum mechanics to perform computations far more efficiently than classical computers. It's crucial for solving complex problems in fields such as cryptography, machine learning, and optimization.
